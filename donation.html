India tries to stay afloat amidst the ‘second wave’ of the pandemic, our poor and vulnerable elders face the highest risk. Countless have lost their lives and much more have lost their earning men of the house. This organisation aims to provide basic ration relief and essential items to these families who are striving hard to survive in this critical situation.

Based on some on-ground survey, below are mentioned the commodities on which the donated funds would be utilised :-

SURVIVAL KITS includes items like rice,wheat, spices, cooking oil, soap, detergent along with Immunity boosters such as Zincovit, Vitamin C and some high quality reusable face masks.


The need on-ground is immense. Your little contribution can help an elder survive longer and bring hope during this uncertain time.
